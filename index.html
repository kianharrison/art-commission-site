TERMS OF SERVICE



  1. Payment: All transactions are handled via PayPal. A 50% deposit is required upfront to start the project. The final work will be delivered once the remaining balance is paid in full.
  2. Pricing: Base prices are subject to change depending on the complexity of the request or additional assets required.
  3. References: Clients must provide clear references (sketches, mood boards, or detailed briefs) to ensure accuracy.
  4. Workflow & Revisions: I will provide progress sketches for approval. You are entitled to 3 rounds of minor revisions. Major changes requested after the sketch is approved may result in extra costs.
  5. Timeline: Art is a creative process. While I aim for our agreed deadline, please allow a 3-day grace period for unforeseen delays.
  6. Usage: For personal use only. Commercial rights must be discussed and purchased separately. I reserve the right to post the completed work to my professional portfolio.
  7. Content Restrictions: I do not accept commissions involving NSFW, heavy gore, or hateful content.
